PROGRAM OBJECTIVE

The Future Founders Program by Ratan Tata Innovation Hub (RTIH), Rajahmundry, is a structured 45-day hybrid accelerator designed to take early-stage entrepreneurs from raw idea to validated prototype. Participants journey through four phases — Ignite, Build, Test, and Launch — gaining hands-on skills in design thinking, business modelling, financial literacy, prototyping, and investor pitching. The program culminates in a public Demo Day where cohort teams present their ventures to industry, government, and investment stakeholders.
